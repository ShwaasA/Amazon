Amazon.inc doing business as Amazon, is an American E-commerce and cloud computing company based in Seattle, Washington that was founded by Jeff Bezos on July 5, 1994. Amazon has separate retail websites for the United States, the United Kingdom and Ireland, France, Canada, Germany, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Australia, Brazil, Japan, China, India, and Mexico. Amazon also offers international shipping of some of its products to certain other countries.
